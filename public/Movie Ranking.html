The Best Movies that I Recommend

My top 3 movies All The Time

Avatar: The Way of Water

This is my Favorite movies all the time. The Way of Water looks fantastic. It’s a technical marvel, showcasing what 13 years, endless amounts of money, and no care about the human cost of overtime crunch can accomplish. It’s a reminder that good 3D can look amazing and immersive.

Star Treck Beyond

A fun popcorn movie that highlights the value of teamwork and a good playlist (priceless core values in life).

Captain America: The First Avenger

To see Captain America in a WWII backdrop battling Red Skull on the big screen is every fanboy’s dream, and Johnston delivers the goods.
